About PMSSS

The Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) is a flagship initiative by the Government of India aimed at empowering the youth of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh by providing financial assistance for higher education. Launched to promote peace and development in the region, PMSSS enables deserving students to pursue undergraduate degrees in recognized institutions across India.The scheme covers tuition fees, maintenance, and other allowances for courses in engineering, medical, and general education, ensuring that talented students from these regions have access to quality education and greater opportunities.
